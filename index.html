EVENTS & FUNCTIONS

Book your next private event, function or work meeting at Plum Tucker Cafe.

Here at Plum Tucker we have the perfect space for entertaining! With enough room to comfortably host between 30 – 50 people our space provides a quiet, cosy and well lit environment perfect for any occasion both day and night. The tables can be arranged to your desire, making it ideal for meetings, presentations, large family get-togethers, art shows, pop up stores and photo shoots!

Be sure to ask us about our specialty grazing tables tailor-made just for you!

If you’re looking to book a larger party or event for the afternoon or evening then you’re more than welcome to reserve the entire cafe! The downstairs courtyard area can host between 50 – 70 people and is equipped with outdoor heaters, keeping it nice and cosy all year round.
